Chicken Caesar Salad

Description

A classic Chicken Caesar Salad featuring crisp romaine lettuce, tender grilled chicken, crunchy croutons, and a creamy homemade Caesar dressing. This restaurant-quality salad is perfect for lunch or dinner!

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 2 large chicken breasts
  • 3 hearts of romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese shavings
  • Fresh black pepper

For the Caesar Dressing:

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 4 anchovy fillets, minced (optional)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Grill or pan-fry until cooked through (165Â°F). Let rest, then slice.
  2. For the dressing:
    • In a bowl, whisk together minced garlic, mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, and lemon juice
    • Add Worcestershire sauce and anchovies (if using)
    • Stir in grated Parmesan cheese
    • Season with salt and pepper to taste
  3. Wash and chop romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces
  4. In a large bowl, toss lettuce with desired amount of dressing
  5. Top with sliced chicken, croutons, and Parmesan shavings
  6. Finish with fresh ground black pepper
  7. Serve immediately while crisp and fresh

Additional Notes

  • Prep Time: 20 minutes
  • Cook Time: 15 minutes
  • Servings: 4
  • Make ahead: Prepare dressing up to 3 days in advance

Tips

  • For extra flavor, marinate the chicken in lemon juice and garlic before cooking
  • Make your own croutons by toasting bread cubes with olive oil and garlic
  • For a lighter version, substitute Greek yogurt for half of the mayonnaise
