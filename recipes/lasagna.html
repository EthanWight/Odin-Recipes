Classic Lasagna

Description

This hearty Italian classic features layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheese. Perfect for feeding a crowd or having delicious leftovers!

Ingredients

  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound Italian sausage
  • 2 (28 oz) cans crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 oz) cans tomato paste
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons dried basil
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 32 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, brown ground beef and Italian sausage. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, garlic, basil, and oregano. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with eggs and 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese.
  5. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. Layer in a 9x13 baking dish:
    • Thin layer of meat sauce
    • Lasagna noodles
    • Ricotta mixture
    • Mozzarella cheese
    • Meat sauce
    • Repeat layers 2-3 times
  7. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  9. Remove foil and bake for additional 25 minutes until bubbly and golden brown.
  10. Let rest for 15 minutes before serving.

Additional Notes

  • Prep Time: 45 minutes
  • Cook Time: 50 minutes
  • Rest Time: 15 minutes
  • Servings: 12
