Chicken Alfredo

Description

A classic Italian-American dish that combines fettuccine pasta with creamy Alfredo sauce and tender chicken breast. This rich and satisfying meal is perfect for family dinners!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fettuccine pasta
  • 2 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook fettuccine according to package instructions. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water.
  2. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Cook in a large skillet until golden brown and cooked through. Slice into strips.
  3. In the same skillet, melt butter and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Cook for 5 minutes until slightly thickened.
  5. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted and sauce is smooth.
  6. Add cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat. Add pasta water if needed to reach desired consistency.
  7. Add sliced chicken and toss to combine.
  8. Garnish with fresh parsley and extra Parmesan cheese.

Additional Notes

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 25 minutes
  • Servings: 4-6
